Fallout With Fallout?

This site makes it easier to follow the Initial Dispute Resolution procedure in the the Zenimax Terms of Service. You can fill and print the form/letter that ZeniMax requires to start their dispute resolution process.

ZeniMax's Terms of Service contain other relevant procedures for dispute resolution through Arbitration. You should read the relevant sections of the Terms of Service before attempting to initiate your dispute using this form.

WHY IS THERE NO ADVICE ON WHAT CLAIMS TO MAKE OR WHAT REMEDY TO ASK FOR?

This site is not for giving legal advice. You should only use this site if you already know what you want the form to say - and just want the convenience of filling the form and/or having us mail it for you.

SELF-SERVICE PRINTING & MAILING:

Step 1: Fill out the form below with your dispute. We recommend writing your dispute in your own word processor and copy/pasting the text into the form. On submission, you'll receive a page with your filled form ready for saving and printing. We recommend saving your form to PDF and printing your form before mailing, even if we mail it for you. Keep copies for yourself as we do not store your information at this stage!

Step 2: Mail your form to the address listed in the Zenimax Terms of Service.

FULL-SERVICE PRINTING & MAILING:

Step 3 (optional): You'll be presented with the option to have us print and mail your form for you for a small fee. We supply the, er, supplies, we do the legwork. You just fill out the form and give us some money for our time and expense.

  1. We will print and mail your dispute to the address provided in the ZeniMax Terms of Service.
  2. First-Class Postal Service mail: $3.00 (limit approx. 4 pages, 15,000 characters).
  3. (Coming soon) Priority Mail with Tracking: $10.00 (limit approx. 30 pages).
  4. Your submission will be stored securely in a Google Spreadsheet behind a Two-Factor-Authenticated account.
  5. You'll receive email confirmation of the date your letter was mailed. Your letter will be mailed as soon as practically possible. Our target is the next business day, but it could be slightly longer as we wait for supplies (toner, envelopes, etc.)
  6. Note: There is currently a limit of four pages total for the final printed mailing. If your dispute is longer than this and you'd like us to mail it, please Contact Us and let us know. We are considering implementing higher page counts and Priority Mail. We currently have this limit in place so we are not overwhelmed with mailing issues. We will grow with your demand! Thank you for your understanding!
